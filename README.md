# AI-VO2 Score Calculator

This repository hosts the official interactive calculator for the **AI-VO2 score**, an AI-derived tool developed to predict significant effort intolerance and adverse clinical outcomes across the heart failure (HF) spectrum.

### 🔗 [Live Calculator](https://nrp88.github.io/AI-VO2-score/)

## Overview
The AI-VO2 score is a parsimonious 4-variable clinical tool designed for rapid, first-line assessment of patients with or at risk for heart failure. It integrates routinely collected clinical and echocardiographic data to identify patients potentially warranting advanced functional assessment.

### Key Predictors
The score is calculated based on four parameters, each mechanistically linked to oxygen transport and cardiac performance:
- **Body Mass Index (BMI)**
- **Haemoglobin**
- **Average LV S′** (Left ventricle systolic mitral annulus tissue velocity)
- **sPAP** (Systolic pulmonary artery pressure)

### Clinical Significance
The AI-VO2 score predicts:
1. **Effort Intolerance:** Defined as a peak oxygen uptake (VO₂) < 16 mL/kg/min according to Weber’s classification.
2. **Prognosis:** Independent prediction of the composite endpoint of heart failure hospitalization or all-cause mortality.

## Risk Stratification
Based on validated thresholds, patients are classified into three risk strata which show a significant prognostic gradient:
- **Low Risk (<10):** Low probability of significant effort intolerance.
- **Intermediate Risk (10–13):** Patients may benefit from closer clinical follow-up and specialized HF evaluation.
- **High Risk (>13):** High probability of impaired exercise capacity; referral for Cardiopulmonary Exercise Testing (CPET) is strongly recommended.
